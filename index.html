BSL Sweep → Short (Trader Zed model) ~68% WR
  • Identify the swing high = Buy Side Liquidity (BSL) on 4H or 1H
  • Watch for wick or close ABOVE BSL — this is the sweep (stop hunt on longs)
  • Wait for CHoCH: first bearish candle to close below a recent swing low = structure shift confirmed
  • Identify FVG created in the shift impulse — mark the 3-candle gap zone
  • Price retraces INTO FVG = IFVG entry trigger. Wait for rejection wick
  • Enter SHORT on wick rejection. SL above BSL. TP = sell-side liquidity (SSL) below
💡 Best at London open 08:00 UTC and NY open 13:00 UTC. Same model fires every day — "Same Model. Same Time. Same Results."
SSL Sweep → Long (Mirror model) ~65% WR
  • Identify swing low = Sell Side Liquidity (SSL) on 1H or 4H
  • Watch for wick below SSL — price grabs stops of longs underneath
  • CHoCH: first bullish close ABOVE previous swing high = confirmed structure shift
  • Mark FVG from the bullish impulse after CHoCH
  • Wait for pullback into FVG (IFVG) with rejection wick
  • Enter LONG on rejection. SL below SSL sweep low. TP = BSL above
💡 Works best when 4H daily bias is bullish. Check for BSL above as target. Risk 5% max.
FVG / IFVG Precision Entry ~72% WR with confluence
  • FVG = 3 candles where Candle1.high and Candle3.low have a price gap (price moved too fast)
  • Bullish FVG: Candle1.high → Candle3.low (gap above). Bearish FVG: Candle1.low → Candle3.high
  • IFVG = price RETURNS into the FVG zone — this triggers your entry, not the FVG itself
  • Wait for rejection wick inside the FVG on 1M or 5M before entering
  • Strongest when FVG overlaps with Order Block. Never enter FVG against 4H trend
  • SL just outside FVG zone. TP = next liquidity level or 2:1 RR min
💡 FVG + OB overlap = "premium zone" — highest probability entries. Combine with BSL/SSL sweep for full confluence.
Expansion Model — 3 Criteria ~70% WR London/NY
  • CRITERIA 1 — Liquidity Grab: Price sweeps a BSL or SSL level. This is your "permission" to trade the reversal. No sweep = no trade.
  • CRITERIA 2 — Structure Shift (CHoCH): After the sweep, wait for the first opposing candle to break a recent swing high/low. Confirms smart money reversed.
  • CRITERIA 3 — FVG at the Shift: The impulse creates a FVG. Price returns to it (IFVG) = your exact entry. SL beyond sweep. TP = opposite liquidity pool.
  • This fires reliably at London open and NY open because MM algorithms run liquidity sweeps at session opens — same time, every day.
  • All 3 criteria must be met for 85%+ confluence signal. Missing any one = wait.
💡 This is the "roulette wheel" pattern — highest algorithmic edge. When all 3 criteria align at session open, this is a near-automatic trade.
